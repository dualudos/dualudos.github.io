WEAPONS

Ametralladoro

An unstoppable machine of destruction. It unleashes continuous bursts of high-velocity projectiles, turning the battlefield into a hell of laser fire. Bombarding your enemies with a relentless rain of energy, none can withstand the pressure of your voracious assault.

Ballesto

With a lethal design, it pierces through enemies in a line, ensuring they fall in groups. Its ability for rapid and precise firing allows you to take down waves of aliens as they approach. Get ready for close combat and show your opponents who’s in charge!

Cohetanio

The weapon of choice for absolute chaos on the battlefield. It fires explosive rockets that devastate any target in their path, leaving a trail of destruction in their wake. Ideal for taking out groups of enemies, get ready to be the terror of the aliens!

Double gun

Capable of aiming and annihilating two enemies at the same time. With an agile movement, dance among the alien waves as your revolvers rain down fire. Each shot is a step closer to victory, and each enemy that falls is a celebration of your skill.

Escopeto

The ultimate weapon for annihilating enemies at close range. With a single shot, unleash a devastating wave of energy that disintegrates anyone daring to come near. Feel the power of each load as you sweep through hordes of aliens in an instant. Let the echo of your shots resonate across the battlefield!

Rifleto

The rifle that never stops. With a continuous stream of laser bullets, it’s perfect for dominating the battlefield at mid-range. Each burst weaves a net of destruction that annihilates your enemies before they can get close. Connect with the energy of the universe and watch them fall, one after another.
